01 · OVERVIEW

The City Remembers You

PawPaw World is a relaxing exploration game — no levels gating the world, no combat, no game-over. You roam a dense, hand-built cyberpunk city as PawPaw, chasing curiosity rather than conflict: collecting, climbing, hacking, racing courier jobs, rescuing kittens, and slowly uncovering the mystery of Line 9, the ghost train, and a scattered city AI.

Design Pillars

  • Exploration over combat. The city is the content; everything rewards curiosity.
  • Soft failure only. Missed timers cut a bonus or reset a streak — they never end the game.
  • Always something to do. Tuned for 5-minute, 20-minute, and completion-length sessions.
  • Atmosphere first. Procedural day/night, weather, city events, and adaptive music carry the mood.
  • Self-contained. No build step, no dependencies, no network, no paid mechanics.

03 · THE CITY

Rooftops · Road · Underground

One continuous, fully walkable world stacked in three layers and split into seven named districts. Buildings open onto ground-floor storefront arcades so the street reads as a single promenade, and a drainpipe on every building links the road to the rooftop network.

04 · CITY LIFE & NPCs

A Street That's Always Busy

The city breathes around you. Thirty-plus pedestrians wander on day/night schedules, raise umbrellas in the rain, and crowd around street performers. Fixed characters run stalls, hand out quests, and trade at vendors, while hover-cars, transit buses, and cargo & police drones move through the lanes overhead.

05 · MISSIONS & TASKS

Things To Chase

Nothing is mandatory, but there's always a thread to pull. Endless courier jobs with streak bonuses, three timed parkour races with medals and ghost replays, hackable terminals that unseal doors and restore the city, story quests, NPC errands, photo challenges, kitten rescues, and roaming data wisps.

06 · GAME PROGRESS TRACKING

Everything Is Measured

Progress is tracked at every grain — a live HUD (zone, time, discovery %, Paw Level & title, collectible counts, rotating challenges), a ten-tab journal, and a District Completion percentage that folds together collectibles, secrets, kittens, shrine, mysteries, upgrades, achievements, photos, courier and race records. All of it auto-saves to localStorage.

⬆

Paw Levels

XP from everything you do; eight titles from Street Kitten to City Legend.

✦

Rotating Challenges

Three always-active goals that escalate in tier as you clear them.

◉

Discovery %

Landmarks, teleports, lore and photos feed a discovery meter.

💾

Auto-save

Collectibles, abilities, races (with ghosts), quests & stats persist locally.

07 · SITUATIONS & EVENTS

A Sky & Grid That Change

A full procedural day/night cycle interpolates a ten-stop sky gradient. Five weather states blend smoothly — clear, light & heavy rain, fog, and storm — while random city events break the routine: power outages, kibble-rain bonuses, wisp swarms, the ghost train, hologram glitches, drone failures, street performances, and security sweeps.

Other events not pictured: wisp swarms, the driverless Line 9 ghost train, hologram glitches, cargo-drone malfunctions, street performances, and corporate security sweeps.

08 · MOVEMENT & TRAVERSAL

Flow Through the City

Sprint, double jump, wall-slide & wall-jump, wall-run, dash, glide, ledge-grab, ladders, drainpipes, and ziplines. Chaining moves builds a Flow combo that pays out in credits — and PawPaw can stop to scratch (press C) just because.

09 · CURIOSITIES & ACTIVITIES

Something On Every Corner

Twenty-four interactive curiosities are scattered across all three layers — each gives kibble, credits, or XP, plays a flavor line, then cools down so you can return. Plus a photo mode, lore readers, and the Paw Shrine.

10 · ECONOMY & META

The Pull That Keeps You Wandering

A Fever state doubles all credits & XP while a high flow combo lives; supply drones drop mystery capsules; a vendor economy and skill tree spend what you earn; fast-travel and a city map keep the big world legible.

11 · TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE

Under the Hood

Eleven plain JavaScript modules on a shared global namespace — no framework, no build tooling. The deterministic city is generated from a single seed, facades are pre-rendered to offscreen canvases, and all audio is synthesized at runtime with WebAudio.

🎲

Deterministic World

A seeded RNG builds an identical 12,800×3,700 city every visit — buildings, ziplines, wires, holes and props all placed procedurally.

🎨

Pre-rendered Facades

Each building's windows rasterize once to an offscreen canvas, keeping the render loop cheap with hundreds of lit windows.

🔊

Procedural Audio

Detuned saw pads through a moving filter, zone-adaptive arpeggios, a beat clock, rain/wind noise, footsteps & purrs — all live.

12 · HOW TO PLAY

Enter the City · Learn the Controls

Open the live game, choose NEW GAME to begin or CONTINUE to resume your browser save, then explore freely. Collect holo-kibble to unlock movement abilities, press E near glowing prompts to interact, and use courier work, races, hacking, photos, curiosities, quests, and kitten rescues to earn credits, XP, upgrades, and District Completion.

Quick Start

  1. Move through the street, climb a drainpipe with W, and reach the rooftop network.
  2. Follow interaction prompts with E; nearly every activity rewards progress.
  3. Open the map with M and the journal with J to choose a goal.
  4. Build flow by chaining jumps, climbs, ziplines, slides, and dashes instead of standing still.
  5. Your progress saves automatically in this browser.
